Title: Study Spots

=== comment ===
Off Campus commons is a great place cuz of all the natural sunlight and white light (they have pretty harsh lighting so it basically forces you to be productive), but I do think you do need to live off-campus in order to get in. Basement of the library is also really nice as well as it has a lot of whiteboards and nice "modern" tables and chairs. In the corner of the basement is also a walled off study space which is highboy sound proof. I highly recommend setting up camp there for a few hours since u can literally BLAST music from ur laptop and literally no one outside the room can hear it. Also, VCC North and VCC South are pretty chill places to work in. I believe in one of those, north or south, i don't remember, you can connect your laptop to the TVs and work with ~4 giant screens (it's pretty neat ngl). Also, there's a room in Sage adjacent to 3300 to the left that has a giant table and colorful chairs where you can connect ur laptop to the projector and work like that (it's been pretty useful for group studying). As an added bonus there are also whiteboards all around the walls there as well.

=== comment ===
When I would study with classmates, we would often walk around the buildings near the 86 field (Ricketts, Troy, Sage, etc.) & CII and find random rooms. There's a surprising amount of variety in the atmosphere depending on what floor and building you're in .
