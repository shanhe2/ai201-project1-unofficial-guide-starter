I will say tho, RPI is pretty nerdy and many people lack social skills, so don't be surprised when it happens.

=== comment ===
I started in covid and it was terrible. The isolation probably heavily traumatized me. However, after covid things are happening more. Joining clubs or greek life is a great way to meet people. Talking to random people is also chill. If you make an effort to socialize you will make friends.
One concern is that you’re often so busy you dont have time for friends. Or, when you do have time your friends are busy. This is an issue I’ve had to deal with, and it is a real problem. But if you put yourself out there there will be people to hang out with.
