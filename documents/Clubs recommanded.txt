Greek life

=== comment ===
Fall rush is now only open to sophomores and higher. Go to Meet the Greeks, which is the Greek life kickoff event, and get as many rush calendars as you can. Go to rush events, talk to brothers of different fraternities, see which ones you click with the best and focus on going to more of their events. If you and the brothers get along well, you’ll likely get a bid to the house and you can formally begin pledging the house.
At the very least, go through fall rush attending as many events as you can. I met a lot of people freshman year that way, and rush events are always a blast

Rocketry is a great time. I did not join till my sophomore year and I really fit in fast. I am a mechanical engineer and I never really did anything with rockets before, and I joined on an off thought that I might meet some cool people. I super did and it turned my RPI experience around.
